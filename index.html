Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our library services.

How can I become a member?

You can become a member by visiting the library during operating hours and filling out a membership application form. Please bring a valid ID and proof of address.

What are the library hours?

Our library hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday, 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays.

Can I borrow e-books?

Yes, we offer a selection of e-books available for borrowing through our online platform. A valid library membership is required to access this service.

Do you have Wi-Fi access?

Yes, free high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the library for members and visitors.

About Us

Kgetho Knowledge Hub is a community-focused library dedicated to providing access to information and fostering a love for learning. We aim to be a central resource for our community.

Our Mission

To empower our community through equitable access to knowledge, comprehensive resources, and a welcoming space that inspires lifelong learning and discovery.

Our Vision

To be a leading hub for information, innovation, and community engagement, fostering intellectual curiosity and personal growth for all.
